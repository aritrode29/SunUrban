The GridUrban
Platform

Flexibility Estimation

Hybrid physics + ML models estimate geo-spatial flexibility of every DER in real-time. Know what can flex, when, and by how much.

Fast Response (4-sec)

Frequency regulation and ancillary services. Sub-second dispatch signals to batteries and inverters for grid stability.

Slow Response (15-min+)

Peak shaving, demand response, and TOU optimization. Coordinate EV charging, HVAC, and storage for economic dispatch.

VPP Packaging

Aggregate distributed assets into market-ready products. Sell into ERCOT ancillary services and capacity markets.

Edge Computing

On-device intelligence for latency-critical decisions. Distributed optimization that works even when cloud is down.

Revenue Sharing

Transparent value split with asset owners. Track grid services revenue in real-time and automate payouts.
