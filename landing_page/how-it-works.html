SunUrban transforms parking lots into clean energy power plants. We install solar canopies that generate power, provide shade, and create multiple revenue streams—all while reducing your energy costs.

Three phases.
One mission.

1

Phase 1

Sell Power to Host

Solar canopies power on-site facilities under long-term PPAs. Hosts save, you earn stable revenue.

  • Fixed PPA (8–10¢/kWh)
  • Host saves 25–35% on bills
  • Quickest to deploy
2

Phase 2

Hybrid: Host + Grid

Surplus energy flows to ERCOT. Batteries store and sell during peak hours for higher returns.

  • Hybrid model: host + grid
  • Battery stores low, sells high
  • 15–25% higher total ROI
3

Phase 3

Virtual Power Plant

Connected canopies act as a distributed network — trading flexibility and stabilizing the grid.

  • Networked energy nodes
  • Real-time optimization
  • Multi-market revenue streams
  • Digital Twin Licensing

The Data Layer: Digital Twin Licensing

Phase 3 includes monetizing valuable data streams

Every SunUrban installation generates valuable data. Our Digital Twin Licensing provides real-time urban data to researchers, city planners, retailers, and insurance companies.
