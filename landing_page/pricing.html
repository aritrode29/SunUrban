How the money actually flows

No retail power sales, no behind‑the‑meter tricks. Austin Energy pays SunUrban for solar; SunUrban pays you a contractually defined site host fee.

Reservation deposit

$5,000

One-time, fully credited

What is this for?
We use a small, refundable deposit to reserve engineering capacity and run interconnection and structural checks with Austin Energy. If you move forward, it is fully applied to your site payment schedule.

Capex & operations

$0

for the site host

What is this for?
SunUrban and our capital partners fund equipment, installation, and ongoing operations & maintenance. You provide the site and easements, not capex.

Energy & host payment

Austin Energy pays us

SunUrban pays you

What is this for?
Austin Energy compensates SunUrban for solar production (e.g., ~11.24¢/kWh < 1 MW, ~8.41¢/kWh ≥ 1 MW). We then share a defined percentage of that revenue back with you as a site host fee—no behind‑the‑meter retail energy sales.
