Industry-Standard Methodology

YieldUrban uses validated, physics-based calculations and industry-standard data sources trusted by solar professionals worldwide.

üåç

NREL NSRDB Irradiance Data

Location-specific solar irradiance data from the National Renewable Energy Laboratory's National Solar Radiation Database (NSRDB). The same data source used by NREL SAM and PVWatts.

Source: NREL NSRDB (nsrdb.nrel.gov)

‚öôÔ∏è

Physics-Based Calculations

Production modeling accounts for tilt angle, azimuth orientation, shading factors, system losses (soiling, wiring, inverter, temperature), and annual degradation. Validated against NREL SAM methodology.

Methodology: NREL SAM, PVWatts, IEC 61724

üìä

Industry-Standard Financial Metrics

IRR, NPV, LCOE, and payback calculations follow standard financial modeling practices. Includes ITC, MACRS depreciation, and 25-year cashflow projections.

Standards: NREL LCOE Calculator, SEIA Financial Models

Accuracy & Validation

Pre-Engineering Accuracy: ¬±5%

Validated against NREL SAM for production estimates. Typical variance: ¬±3-7% for annual production, ¬±5-10% for financial metrics (due to site-specific factors not yet surveyed).

Transparent Assumptions

All inputs, calculations, and assumptions are documented in the PDF report. System losses, degradation rates, and financial parameters use industry-standard defaults (customizable).

Pre-Engineering Disclaimer

This is a pre-engineering feasibility analysis. Final engineering studies (geotechnical, structural, interconnection) are required before construction. Actual results may vary ¬±5-10%.
